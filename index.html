Derrick Rose

The man with the unfinished job

Derrick Martell Rose is an American professional basketball player. Here are some of the notable achievements of Derrick Rose:

  • 1988 - Born in Englewood, Chicago
  • 2008 - Drafted to Chicago Bulls
  • 2009 - NBA Rookie of the Year
  • 2011 - Youngest MVP
  • 2012 Dramatic decline - Rose tore his ACL vs. 76ers
  • 2013 - Just getting back from his previous injuries, he tears his right meniscus making him fall short of the remainder of the season.
  • 2016 - Got traded to New York Knicks
  • 2018 - Signed with Cavaliers on a minimum contract
  • 2019 - Traded to Timberwolves on a non-minimum contract
